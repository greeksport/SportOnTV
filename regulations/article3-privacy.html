Article 3: Privacy Policy

1.
Introduction
SportOnTV is committed to protecting user privacy and adhering to relevant data protection laws. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of data collected, how it is used, shared, and protected. By accessing or using SportOnTV, users agree to the terms set forth in this policy.
2.
Data Collection
SportOnTV may collect the following types of data:
  • Non-Personal Data: Information collected automatically, such as IP addresses, browser types, device details, and general site usage analytics. This data helps improve site functionality and user experience.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: SportOnTV may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience, analyze site traffic, and deliver personalized content and advertisements. Users can control cookie preferences through their browser settings.
  • Third-Party Advertiser Data: Ads displayed on SportOnTV may collect data through embedded tracking tools. These third-party providers are responsible for their own data policies, and SportOnTV has no control over their data collection practices.
3.
Data Use
The data collected by SportOnTV is used for the following purposes:
  • Site Functionality and Improvement: Data helps SportOnTV understand user needs and optimize website performance and usability.
  • Ad Personalization and Targeting: Non-personal data may be shared with advertising partners to display relevant ads, improving user experience and supporting the site's operations.
  • Security and Compliance: Information is used to protect the website, monitor for fraudulent activity, and comply with legal obligations.
4.
Data Sharing
SportOnTV does not sell or share personal data with third parties, except in the following instances:
  • Service Providers: Third-party partners, such as analytics or hosting providers, who assist in website functionality. These partners operate under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, SportOnTV may disclose information to comply with legal proceedings, prevent fraud, or protect the rights and safety of users and the platform.
5.
Third-Party Links and Ads
SportOnTV may feature links or advertisements leading to external websites. SportOnTV does not control these third-party sites and is not responsible for their privacy practices or content. Users are encouraged to review the privacy policies of any external websites they visit.
6.
User Rights
SportOnTV respects user rights regarding data privacy:
  • Access and Correction: Users may request access to any personal data SportOnTV may hold and request corrections if the data is inaccurate.
  • Opt-Out and Deletion: Users can opt out of non-essential data collection, such as cookies, through browser settings or request deletion of personal data where applicable.
7.
Data Security
SportOnTV takes reasonable steps to protect user data from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no online transmission is entirely secure, and SportOnTV cannot guarantee absolute data security.
8.
Changes to Privacy Policy
SportOnTV reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Updates will be posted on the website, and users are encouraged to review the policy periodically.
9.
Contact Information
For any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, users may contact SportOnTV through the provided contact channels on the website.
