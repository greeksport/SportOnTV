Article 1: DMCA Compliance and Copyright Infringement Notice

1.
Content Hosting and Responsibility
The streams and content accessible via this website ("SportOnTV") are neither hosted, produced, nor broadcast by SportOnTV. All content is sourced from publicly accessible resources and third-party providers, over which SportOnTV has no control or proprietary rights. SportOnTV operates solely as an indexing service, providing links to external content and embedding video players available on the open web.
2.
Disclaimer of Ownership and Liability
SportOnTV does not claim any ownership over, nor assume responsibility for, any content displayed via embedded links or streams on the platform. SportOnTV serves as a directory for publicly available media and does not engage in, or endorse, the unauthorized sharing or distribution of copyrighted content. Any content found through SportOnTV is the property of its respective owners and may be subject to their own terms of use and copyright policies.
3.
DMCA Compliance and Takedown Requests
SportOnTV respects the intellectual property rights of content creators and complies fully with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA). If any content accessed through links or embedded streams on SportOnTV is believed to infringe upon copyright, SportOnTV encourages copyright holders to address their complaints directly to the hosting entities or streaming platforms responsible for the content.
4.
Procedures for Notification
If a copyright holder wishes to submit a DMCA takedown notice, SportOnTV will review complaints forwarded to the platform's designated DMCA agent. While SportOnTV cannot directly remove or alter third-party content, it will assess any legitimate notice for further action regarding removal or delinking of specific content.
