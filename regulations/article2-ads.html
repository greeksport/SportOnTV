Article 2: Advertisements and Third-Party Content Disclaimer

1.
Advertising Content Disclaimer
SportOnTV displays advertisements as part of its effort to provide a free and accessible service to users. These advertisements are sourced through third-party advertising networks and providers, and are presented through embedded iframes and similar technologies. SportOnTV does not directly host, manage, or monitor the content of these advertisements.
2.
Limitation of Responsibility and Liability
SportOnTV does not accept responsibility for the nature, accuracy, or appropriateness of any advertising content displayed on the platform. This includes, but is not limited to, advertisements that may feature adult content, gambling, malicious software, misleading offers, or other potentially harmful or inappropriate material. Users engaging with advertisements displayed on SportOnTV do so at their own discretion and risk, as SportOnTV does not vet or verify the security or reliability of third-party advertisements.
3.
Third-Party Ad Networks
The advertisements presented on SportOnTV are provided by external ad networks and are managed under their respective policies and terms of service. These third-party ad providers may utilize tracking technologies, cookies, or other methods to deliver targeted ads or gather analytics. SportOnTV does not control or endorse the practices of these providers and encourages users to review the privacy policies and terms of use associated with any advertisements they engage with.
4.
User Discretion and Safety
SportOnTV urges users to exercise caution when interacting with any advertisement, particularly those which may redirect to external sites or request personal information. Users are responsible for protecting their devices and personal data by employing standard security measures, such as maintaining updated antivirus software, being aware of phishing attempts, and avoiding interactions with suspicious ads or links.
5.
Reporting Inappropriate or Harmful Ads
While SportOnTV does not control the specific content of third-party ads, the platform values the safety and satisfaction of its users. Any user who encounters advertisements they believe to be inappropriate, harmful, or in violation of applicable laws or ethical standards is encouraged to report these instances. SportOnTV will endeavor to forward complaints to the respective advertising networks for review, though it cannot guarantee specific outcomes or resolutions.
6.
Indemnification and Waiver
By using SportOnTV, users acknowledge and agree that SportOnTV is not liable for any damages or losses arising from interactions with third-party advertisements. Users agree to indemnify and hold SportOnTV harmless from any claims, losses, or damages resulting from engagement with advertisements displayed on the platform.
